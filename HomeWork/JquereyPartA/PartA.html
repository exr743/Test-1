Chocklate Chip Cookies

This is the best chocolate chip cookie recipe ever! No funny ingredients, no chilling time, etc. Just a simple, straightforward, amazingly delicious, doughy yet still fully cooked, chocolate chip cookie that turns out perfectly every single time!

Summary

  • Prep:20 min
  • Cook: 10 min
  • Servings: 24

    What you need?

  • 1 Cup of butter
  • 1 cup of White Sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 Teaspoons Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Teaspoon baking soda
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of semisweet choclate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Mix together butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat eggs in while adding vanilla extract.
  4. Disolve baking soda into hot water, add to batter with salt.
  5. Stir in flour and choclate chips
  6. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.
  7. Bake for about 10 minutes then Enjoy!
